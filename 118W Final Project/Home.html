About Us

Why we went with the name Chapters is because life is full of different chapters from beginning, middle and eventually an end. In every chapter of every story people grow and learn from experiences within the moments they had in each one.

Life is its own unwritten book and is full of different chapters, and in each way is always a new beginning, new experiences in moments we live for, we want to express every moment or experience into pieces in each season we release. Here we start the beginning of our story, Chapter One.
